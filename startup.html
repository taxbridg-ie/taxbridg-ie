Sole Trader vs. Limited Company

This is the first big decision you'll make. We explain the pros and cons of each:

  • Sole Trader: Easier to set up, but you are personally liable for debts. Profits are taxed at Income Tax rates.
  • Limited Company (LTD): Separate legal entity, protects your personal assets. Profits are taxed at Corporation Tax rates (12.5%). More ongoing compliance required.
