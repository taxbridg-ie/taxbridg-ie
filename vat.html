VAT Services for Irish Businesses

Navigating Value Added Tax can be complex. We simplify registration, returns, and compliance for you.

Do You Need to Register for VAT?

VAT registration is mandatory if your annual turnover exceeds certain thresholds:

  • €37,500 for the supply of services.
  • €75,000 for the supply of goods.
  • Intra-Community Acquisitions over €41,000.

You can also choose to register voluntarily to reclaim VAT on business purchases, which can be beneficial for B2B companies.

VAT Returns & Compliance

Once registered, you must file VAT returns and pay any liability to Revenue. The frequency is typically every two months (VAT 3).

Our VAT Services:

  • Registration: Handling the TR1 or TR2 forms to get your VAT number.
  • VAT 3 Returns: Preparing and filing your periodic returns on ROS.
  • RTD (Return of Trading Details): Filing the annual statistical return required by Revenue.
  • Rates Advice: Clarifying which rate applies to your products/services (Standard 23%, Reduced 13.5%, Second Reduced 9%, etc.).

Cash Receipts Basis

For smaller businesses (turnover under €2m), we can help you apply for the "Cash Receipts Basis," meaning you only pay VAT to Revenue when you actually get paid by your customers, improving your cash flow.
