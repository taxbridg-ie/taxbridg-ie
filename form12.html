Form 12 Submission Service

Maximize your tax refund as a PAYE worker in Ireland. We help you claim every credit you're entitled to.

What is Form 12?

Form 12 is the annual tax return for employees, pensioners, and people with non-PAYE income less than €5,000. While many PAYE workers assume their taxes are sorted by their employer, thousands of Euro in tax relief go unclaimed every year.

Why File a Form 12?

Filing a Form 12 allows you to claim tax back on expenses and reliefs that aren't automatically applied to your payroll.

Common Tax Reliefs We Claim For You:

  • Medical Expenses: 20% relief on GP visits, prescriptions, consultant fees, and non-routine dental care.
  • Flat Rate Expenses: Tax deductions for specific professions (nurses, teachers, tradespeople, etc.) to cover uniform and equipment costs.
  • Rent Tax Credit: Up to €750 (single) or €1,500 (married) for 2024/2025.
  • Tuition Fees: Relief on qualifying third-level fees.
  • Work from Home Relief: Relief for electricity, heating, and broadband if you work remotely.
