Income Tax Filing (Form 11)

Expert support for Self-Employed, Landlords, and Directors. Avoid the October deadline stress.

Who Needs to File a Form 11?

In Ireland, you must register for Income Tax and file a Form 11 if you are a "chargeable person." This typically includes:

  • Self-employed individuals (Sole Traders).
  • Landlords receiving rental income.
  • Proprietary Directors (owning more than 15% of a company).
  • PAYE workers with non-PAYE income over €5,000 net.
  • Recipients of foreign income or dividends.

Key Dates & Obligations

The deadline for filing your tax return and paying the balance of tax is October 31st of the following year (extended to mid-November if filing and paying online via ROS).

Late filing results in automatic surcharges:

  • 5% surcharge (max €12,695) for filing within 2 months of the deadline.
  • 10% surcharge (max €63,485) for filing more than 2 months late.
