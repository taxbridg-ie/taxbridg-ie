Payroll Services for Employers

Accurate, timely, and compliant payroll processing under the PAYE Modernisation system.

Simplifying Payroll for Your Business

Since the introduction of PAYE Modernisation, employers must report payroll details to Revenue on or before every pay date. Failing to do so results in penalties. We take this burden off your shoulders.

Our Payroll Services Include:

  • Revenue Payroll Notifications (RPNs): Retrieving the latest tax credits and cut-off points for each employee before running payroll.
  • Processing Payslips: Calculating Net Pay, PAYE, USC, and PRSI deductions accurately.
  • Payslip Distribution: Securely emailing payslips to your staff (password protected).
  • Revenue Submissions: Uploading the PSR (Payroll Submission Request) to Revenue every pay period.
  • Monthly Returns: Advising you on the total tax liability to be paid to Revenue by the 23rd of the following month.

Managing Employee Benefits

We also handle calculations for Benefit-in-Kind (BIK) on company cars, health insurance, and other non-cash benefits, ensuring you remain fully compliant.
